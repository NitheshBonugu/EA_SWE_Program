Here are the steps that we will need to take:

1.	Install Unreal Engine. Keep in mind you must have a compatible system.
2.	Launch Unreal Project Browser.
3.	Choose Blueprint / C++ Template.
4.	Choose the device: Desktop/Console or Mobile/Tablet.
5.	Choose Max Quality or Scalable 2D/3D.
6.	Store the Project.
7.	Open the Project.

And now, you can carry on with your project, and play with it.
